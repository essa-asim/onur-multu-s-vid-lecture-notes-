## General Purpose vs Special Purpose Systems
- Microprocessor -> General purpose architecture  
- FPGA -> Reconfigurable architecture  
- ASIC -> Fixed-function architecture  

---

## Transistors
- MOS transistor  
- n-type & p-type  
- Transistor as a switch  
- nMOS + pMOS = CMOS  

---

## CMOS Transistor
- p-type -> good at pulling up voltage  
- n-type -> good at pulling down voltage  

---

## Logic Gates (CMOS)
- CMOS NOT gate (inverter)  
- CMOS NAND gate  
  - pMOS -> parallel  
  - nMOS -> series  

- NAND -> AND by using NOT with NAND  

> NAND is a universal gate.
